# Module 3: Direct Fan Subscriptions & Pay-Per-View

If social media is the "top of the funnel," direct fan subscriptions are the bottom. This is where you monetize your most dedicated followers. Because fans are paying you directly, the revenue per viewer here is drastically higher than algorithmic ad splits. 

The platforms in this module require a mental shift: you are no longer chasing millions of views; you are optimizing for customer lifetime value (LTV) and monthly recurring revenue (MRR).

---

## 1. OnlyFans: The Industry Giant

OnlyFans remains the undisputed heavyweight of direct-to-fan monetization. With billions in annual fan spend, it possesses the highest consumer trust for inputting credit card information.

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Revenue Split:** A flat 20% platform fee (creators keep 80% of all earnings). 
*   **Income Streams:** Monthly subscriptions, Pay-Per-View (PPV) messages, feed tips, and custom content requests.
*   **Structure:** Accounts typically have a single subscription price point. To offer a "free tier," creators usually must create a second, separate account.

### 💡 Elite Strategy: The "Freemium to PPV" Funnel
Top earners on OnlyFans do not make the majority of their money from the monthly subscription fee—they make it in the Direct Messages (DMs). The elite strategy is to keep the monthly subscription price low (e.g., $4.99–$9.99) to get as many fans "in the door" as possible. Once inside, creators use highly targeted, mass-sent PPV messages. A $5 subscriber who buys two $20 PPV videos a month is far more valuable than a subscriber paying a $20 flat monthly fee.

---

## 2. Patreon: The Membership Pioneer

Patreon is the gold standard for podcasters, YouTubers, artists, and educators who want to offer tiered perks to their community without the heavy focus on PPV messaging.

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Platform Fee:** 10% platform fee (for creators joining after August 2025). 
*   **Hidden Costs:** When adding payment processing (~3%), currency conversion, and payout fees, the true effective fee is usually between **12% and 15%**. (Apple iOS in-app purchases also add a hefty surcharge).
*   **Structure:** Highly customizable multi-tier memberships (e.g., $5 Bronze, $10 Silver, $25 Gold) with automated Discord role integrations.

### 💡 Elite Strategy: The "Discord Anchor"
Patreon churn (subscribers canceling) is notoriously high if the only perk is "bonus content." Elite creators tie their Patreon tiers directly to access in a private Discord server. When fans pay for Patreon, they are actually paying for proximity to the creator and the community. If they cancel, they lose their friends in the Discord. This dramatically increases retention rates.

---

## 3. Fansly: The Multi-Tier Innovator

Fansly emerged as a direct competitor to OnlyFans but built the features that creators had been begging for, most notably the ability to have multiple subscription tiers on a single page.

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Revenue Split:** 20% platform fee (creators keep 80%).
*   **Structure:** You can offer a Free tier, a $9.99 tier, and a $49.99 VIP tier all on the exact same profile.
*   **Referrals:** A lifetime 5% referral bonus for bringing other creators to the platform (unlike OnlyFans, which caps referrals at 12 months).

### 💡 Elite Strategy: The Seamless Upsell
Because Fansly allows a free tier and a premium tier on the same page, you don't lose traffic bouncing between two different links. The strategy is to post heavily on the Free tier with watermarked or blurred previews. Fans see exactly what they are missing and can upgrade to the premium tier with a single click without ever leaving your profile.

---

## 4. Fanvue: The High-Growth Challenger

Fanvue is rapidly gaining market share by offering a cleaner user interface, better discoverability, and tools designed for both traditional creators and AI-generated models.

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Revenue Split:** 20% platform fee (creators keep 80%).
*   **Payouts:** Known for rapid payouts and modern withdrawal options, including a 1% fee via TripleA crypto withdrawals.
*   **Discoverability:** Unlike OnlyFans, Fanvue has built-in discovery feeds to help fans find new creators organically.

### 💡 Elite Strategy: Pricing for Conversion
Fanvue creators succeed by optimizing their pricing brackets. Data shows that starting with a "Mid-Tier" price ($14.99) allows you to gauge demand. If conversion is low but retention is high, you lower the entry price and focus on upsells. If conversion is high but retention drops, your price is too low and attracting uncommitted buyers—raise the price by 20% to stabilize your MRR.

3. Script your first high-ticket PPV message to send to your entry-level subscribers.
