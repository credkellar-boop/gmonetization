# Module 1: Video & Live Streaming

Video is the highest-leverage medium for building a deeply engaged audience. The platforms in this module function as the "top of the funnel" for most creators, allowing you to capture attention and monetize it through algorithmic distribution and live interaction.

---

## 1. YouTube: The Evergreen Engine

YouTube is the undisputed king of long-form video. Unlike standard social media where content dies after 48 hours, a well-optimized YouTube video can generate ad revenue for years.

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Ad Revenue Split (Long-Form):** 55% to the creator / 45% to YouTube.
*   **Ad Revenue Split (Shorts):** 45% to the creator / 55% to YouTube (pooled based on views).
*   **Alternative Income:** Channel Memberships (tiered monthly subscriptions), Super Chats/Stickers (live stream donations), and YouTube Shopping integration.
*   **YPP Requirements:** 1,000 subscribers AND either 4,000 public watch hours (long-form) OR 10 million Shorts views in the last 90 days.

### 💡 Elite Strategy: The "Hero, Hub, Help" Framework
Do not rely purely on Adsense. Use broad, highly-edited "Hero" content to capture viral attention. Use recurring "Hub" content (like a weekly podcast format) to build parasocial loyalty. Use "Help" content (search-optimized tutorials) for slow, compounding daily views. Push your most loyal viewers into Channel Memberships or off-platform digital products.

---

## 2. Twitch: The Live Community Standard

Twitch relies on extreme watch time and real-time community interaction. It is less about virality and more about habitual, daily viewership. 

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Subscriptions:** Tier 1 ($4.99), Tier 2 ($9.99), Tier 3 ($24.99). The standard split is 50/50, though top creators in the Partner Plus program can unlock a 70/30 split.
*   **Bits (Tips):** 1 Bit = 1 cent to the creator. Twitch takes its cut when the viewer *purchases* the Bits, meaning creators keep 100% of the tip value.
*   **Ad Incentive Program:** Creators can run scheduled ad breaks for a guaranteed flat payout or a 55% share of ad revenue.

### 💡 Elite Strategy: Off-Platform Funneling
Twitch has virtually zero algorithmic discoverability for new creators. The defining strategy for Twitch growth is creating viral clips on TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and X, and directly funneling that traffic to your live Twitch broadcasts. 

---

## 3. Kick: The High-Margin Challenger

Kick entered the market specifically targeting Twitch's pain points—namely, low revenue splits and strict content moderation. 

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Subscription Split:** A market-disrupting **95% to the creator / 5% to Kick**.
*   **Kicks (Tips):** Similar to Bits, but integrated with Stripe for rapid payouts.
*   **Creator Incentive Program (CIP):** An hourly payout system where Kick literally pays creators an hourly wage based on viewer count, chat engagement, and stream frequency.

### 💡 Elite Strategy: Subscription Arbitrage
Because of the 95/5 split, a subscriber on Kick is worth nearly double a standard subscriber on Twitch. Savvy creators use Kick not for discoverability, but as the final destination for their most hardcore, paying fans. Transitioning a 500-subscriber community from Twitch to Kick effectively doubles your monthly recurring revenue (MRR) overnight.
