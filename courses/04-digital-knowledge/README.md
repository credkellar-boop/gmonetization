# Module 4: Digital Products, Courses & Communities

When you transition from selling *access* to selling *knowledge*, your revenue potential scales infinitely. Digital products have near-zero marginal cost of reproduction. The platforms in this module allow you to package your expertise into downloads, courses, and premium communities.

---

## 1. Gumroad: The Frictionless Storefront

Gumroad is the absolute lowest-friction way to sell a digital download. It is favored by creators selling templates, eBooks, software presets, and simple video workshops.

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Pricing Structure (Updated 2026):** Gumroad charges no monthly subscription fee. Instead, they take a flat **10% + $0.50** per direct transaction. 
*   **Payment Processing:** Standard credit card processing (~2.9% + $0.30) is charged *on top* of the 10%, meaning the effective fee is roughly 13% for direct sales.
*   **Discover Marketplace:** If a buyer finds your product via Gumroad's internal search/recommendations rather than your own link, Gumroad takes a **30% cut**.
*   **Merchant of Record:** Gumroad automatically handles global VAT and sales tax, saving creators massive administrative headaches.

### 💡 Elite Strategy: The "Pay What You Want" Lead Magnet
Because there are no monthly fees, Gumroad is the ultimate email capture tool. Elite creators offer a high-value digital product (like a Notion template) for "$0+" (Pay what you want). This removes all friction for the buyer, builds a massive customer email list, and surprisingly, 5-10% of users will voluntarily pay a few dollars for a "free" product.

## 2. Teachable: The Course Powerhouse

When a simple video download isn't enough, Teachable provides a full Learning Management System (LMS) with student tracking, quizzes, and certificates.

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Pricing Structure (Updated 2026):** Teachable no longer offers a free plan. The base "Starter" tier is $29/month (billed annually) plus a **7.5% transaction fee** on every sale.
*   **Scaling Up:** The "Builder" tier is $69/month (annually) but drops the platform transaction fee to **0%**.
*   **Hidden Limits:** Starter caps you at 5 published products and 100 students, pushing successful creators to upgrade quickly.

### 💡 Elite Strategy: The Break-Even Upgrade
Do not stay on the Starter plan once your course gains traction. The math is simple: at roughly $550/month in course sales, the 7.5% transaction fee on the Starter plan costs more than the price difference to upgrade to the Builder plan. Once you hit $500/mo in revenue, immediately upgrade to the $69/mo tier to lock in 0% fees and maximize your profit margins.

## 3. Mighty Networks: The Premium Community

Courses have notoriously low completion rates. Communities, however, have high engagement and recurring revenue. Mighty Networks allows you to build a private social network that you fully own and control.

### The Monetization Mechanics
*   **Structure:** You pay a flat monthly SaaS fee (starting around $41/month for the Community plan or $119/month for the Courses plan).
*   **Transaction Fees:** Mighty Networks charges a very low 3% platform fee on subscriptions and one-time purchases (plus standard Stripe processing).
*   **White-Labeling:** You can run the community on your own custom domain, entirely escaping social media algorithms.

### 💡 Elite Strategy: The "Course-as-a-Community" Flip
Instead of selling a static $500 video course on Teachable, elite creators use Mighty Networks to sell a $50/month "Academy." The videos are included, but the true value is the live weekly Q&A calls, peer-to-peer networking, and direct feedback. By shifting from a one-time course to a recurring community, the Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) frequently doubles or triples.
